Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty period for your products?

All our products come with a 2-year warranty. Out-of-box defects are replaced immediately, and any other issues are repaired free of cost within the warranty period.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

For onsite requests, we respond within 48 hours. For issues that can be resolved remotely via phone or computer, we provide support within 24 hours.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide professional commissioning and installation services. Our expert team ensures proper setup and provides training for end-users.

What are your payment terms?

We require 70% advance payment, with the balance payable against the proforma invoice. Delivery is typically 1-2 weeks from the date of purchase order.

Which brands do you deal in?

We are authorized dealers for premium brands including Hikvision, CP-Plus, Dahua, Panasonic, Honeywell, Samsung, Godrej, and many more leading security equipment manufacturers.

Do you provide customized security solutions?

Absolutely! We provide tailored security solutions based on your specific requirements, budget, and premises layout. Contact us for a free consultation.
